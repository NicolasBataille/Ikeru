## Context

Ikeru is a personal Japanese learning app built in SwiftUI. It uses FSRS spaced repetition for card reviews, with an RPG gamification layer (XP, levels, loot drops, equipped cosmetics). It's dark-mode only, designed for iPhone.

## Design Direction (established from day one)

The core aesthetic is **wabi-sabi** — the Japanese philosophy of imperfect, weathered, quiet beauty:

- **Sumi ink black** backgrounds (#0A0A0F) — deep, warm-cool, never pure black
- **Kintsugi gold** as primary accent (#D4A574) — the art of repairing with gold, embracing imperfection
- **Sakura pink** (#E8B4B8) and **matcha green** (#7A8471) as supporting tones
- **Washi paper white** text (#F5F2EC) — warm, never pure white
- **Glass surfaces** — translucent layers with white at 6% fill, 12% stroke, 18% highlight. Cards float above the sumi background with soft warm shadows
- **Negative space as a design element** — generous spacing (16/24/36pt), the calm comes from what's NOT there
- **Movement through stillness** — animations are calm springs (0.28 response, 0.86 damping), never bouncy or aggressive
- **NotoSerifJP** for kanji display, SF Pro for system text. Tight display tracking (-1.2), open micro tracking (+0.8)

The rarity system uses coordinated colors: common (#8A8780 grey), rare (#6B92B5 dusk blue), epic (#9580B5 murasaki purple), legendary (#D4A574 gold — same as primary, earned).

Full token reference:
- Backgrounds: #0A0A0F, elevated #12121A, surface #18181F, surface-elevated #1F1F28
- Success #8FBCA0 (moss), Warning #E0A062 (amber), Danger #C97064 (terracotta)
- Text secondary #B8B5B0, tertiary #7A7770
- Radius: sm 12, md 18, lg 24, xl 32, capsule 9999
- Shadows: card (black 45%, r24, y8), glow (gold 25%, r32), elevated (black 60%, r40, y12)
